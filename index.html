Welcome to Bitcoin

The information needed to use and participate in Bitcoin is relatively low and most people with basic technology skills can pick up on Bitcoin quickly. Bitcoin is a cash system on the internet that operates peer-to-peer across international borders or locally for a lower fee than most sytems we have in place now. The requirements to participate in this system are a smartphone, internet connection and a simple understanding of the application you store your Bitcoin on your smartphone.

I am NOT going to tell you about the history, other than a few key items I will not explain how bitcoin works or use major words most people do not understand, and I will not explain how bitcoin has risks or about you becoming and investor, sort of. If you are looking at how to use Bitcoin, I am assuming you know how it works to some degree and you have made the informed choice to participate. I feel there is a disconnect between us nerds and the everyday people when explaining how to use this simple network. Using the network does not require the background knowledge most people go into depth while explaining to new users; learning about Bitcoin serves more as an additional hobby to learn about how the neat new technology works, but is unnecessary when it comes to using the network day to day, which we need in order to make it stronger and more successful.

The purpose of this website is to explain how to store bitcoin, how to buy your first bitcoin, where to send bitcoin, and how to spend Bitcoin. I will also throw some facts about Bitcoin on the right sidebar.
